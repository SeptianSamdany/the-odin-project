Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian pasta dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, meat, and tomato sauce.

This hearty dish is perfect for family dinners and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente.
  2. Cook ground beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  3. Layer pasta, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Bake in the oven until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Serve hot.
