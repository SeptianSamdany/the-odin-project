Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a quick and delicious dish made by stir-frying rice with vegetables, eggs, and seasonings.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables (carrot, peas, corn)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok.
  2. Scramble eggs and set aside.
  3. Stir-fry garlic and vegetables.
  4. Add rice and soy sauce, then mix well.
  5. Add eggs back in, stir, and serve.
