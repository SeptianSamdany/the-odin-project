Pancake

Description

Pancakes are soft, fluffy breakfast treats served with syrup, butter, or fruits.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Whisk milk and eggs together, then combine with dry ingredients.
  3. Heat a pan and add butter.
  4. Pour batter and cook until bubbles appear.
  5. Flip and cook the other side, then serve.
